Trusted and Established

Since 2004 Firstview Lettings Ltd has been providing residential property letting and management services to landlords and tenants. Helping tenants find homes and flats to rent and helping landlords effectively manage their properties.

Reliable and Ready

We understand the importance of having hands-on and accesible landlords. Having over 15 years of experience to call upon, we can help both landlords and tenants navigate the letting process.

Comprehensive Services

Offering a range of landlord and tenant services, we're able to best support the varied lifestyles of both investment landlords and experienced landlords, helping them to achieve their goals.

We offer our services in Nottinghamshire and Derbyshire's rural countryside towns and villages and work with landlords in the area to provide a choice of homes and apartments to rent.
