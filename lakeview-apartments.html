About this property

Enjoy a high standard of living with this modern one-bedroom apartment with fitted hob and built-in oven. Located within walking distance of local shops and amenities. A double bedroom, large lounge area and seperate kitchen provide a comfortable amount of living space. Neutrally decorated with carpet throughout, double glazing and electric shower. Energy efficient Economy7 heating with night storage heaters ensures a warm home all year round.

Local Services & Amenities:

Lakeview apartments are situated within walking distance of the busy Fishponds shopping area. Tesco supermarket, petrol station and Ikea store is 1 mile away on junction 2 of the M32. This junction is also the access point to the M5/M4 motorway network just 2 miles away. The Cribbs Causeway shopping centre, which includes restaurants and multi theatre cinema complex is within a 30 minute drive on junction 16 of the M5.
